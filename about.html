About

At Myee, we use our extensive industry knowledge and network to deliver the best possible outcome for our client. We do this by taking the time to understand their vision and walking them through each step to achieving it.

With an impressive portfolio of houses, duplexes, granny flats, apartments and commercial properties, we’ve worked with all budgets and requirements. We use this experience to guarantee a better build at a better price.
  1. PROFESSIONALISM - To understand your vision, we first need to understand you. Above all things, communication is key, so we take the time to have lengthy conversations with you and use the learnings in our conduct. 
  2. INTEGRITY - If we say something, we mean it. We want you to trust us, so while we deliver on what we promise, we’ll also let you know when things aren’t right. We emphasise full transparency over the span of the build.
  3. QUALITY - The proof is in the pudding. A quality build is only possible through quality service. You’ll love working with us and it’ll show in the final product – a beautifully built property that will stand the test of time.
